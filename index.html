Nómada

Nómada is a set of furniture that is made up of a rest, work and storage elements. The purpose of this is to issue a furniture easy to assemble and dissasemble, easy to transport, economical, functional and charged of symbolism. They're designed under the open design philosophy, each piece of furniture having it's own instructions downloadable just here! If you have any questions, please, contact me!
«It's a conscious and socialy responsible project, focusing on the material dispossession which Victor Papanek speaks.»
